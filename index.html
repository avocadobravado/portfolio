Hi, I'm Rose Sponseller,
a UX Designer.

I just like making stuff.

I'm a Portland-based UX designer. The internet and I go way back; one of the first websites I made was a Sailor Moon fan club on GeoCities. I've worked in tech for 10 years, starting in customer support. I became interested in UX because I cared for the people who used our products and wanted to create solutions to their problems. I am drawn to the interactions between people and technology and emotional responses to design. Outside of work, I'm probably knitting, avoiding the sun, or doting on my cat.
