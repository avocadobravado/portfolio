About Penelopie

Penelopie is a site for a fictitious bakery located in downtown Flint, Michigan (my hometown!). The site features every flavor of pie the bakery offers, location and contact information, information about the bakery, and a contact form.

Process

Built over the span of one day, I made Penelopie as a personal challenge to myself to create a site in a few hours that looked modern, professional, and would fit the needs of a small business. At the time the site was made (May 2017), I had only been using Bootstrap for 4 weeks. All the images used on the site are from Unsplash.
