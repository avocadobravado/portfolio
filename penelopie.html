About Penelopie

Penelopie is a site for a fictitious bakery located in downtown Flint, Michigan (my hometown!). Built over the span of one day, I made Penelopie as a personal challenge to myself to create a site in a few hours that looked modern, professional, and would fit the needs of a small business. At the time the site was made (May 2017), I had only been using Bootstrap for 4 weeks.
