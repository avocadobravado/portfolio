About Rose City Zoo

Rose City Zoo is a web application built with AngularJS and TypeScript with full CRUD capabilities. The site has two purposes: the first is to display the zoo's animal information in a child-friendly way, with bright colors, playful animations, and simple language. The second is for zoo staff to be able to manage the site's information, which includes editing animal data (name, age, likes, dislikes, etc.), as well as adding new and deleting animals from the application.
