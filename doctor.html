About docSearch PDX

Are you in need of a doctor? With docSearch PDX, you can simply type in a symptom or ailment (e.g., heart disease or sore throat) to find a list of doctors in the Portland, Oregon who can treat the issue, along with their specialties, addresses, phone numbers, and biography. All the data displayed in the search results are retrieved from the BetterDoctor API.

Process

docSearchPDX was built in the span of one day with JavaScript, jQuery, Materialize, and the BetterDoctor API (JSON format). The app's dependencies are managed with npm, Gulp, and Bower. Some of the dependencies for docSearchPDX include concatenation and minification, Browserify, a linter (JS Hint), and live reloading (Browsersync — which I can no longer live without!). This workflow allowed me to get into a rhythm of developing the app efficiently and identify issues quickly, thanks to the linter. This was also one of my first experiences with an API, so I appreciated BetterDoctor's easy-to-read documentation.
